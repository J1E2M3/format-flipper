Privacy

Effective July 6, 2026 · applies to the Format Flipper tool at toolymctoolface.com/format/

The short version: the data you paste into Format Flipper is never uploaded anywhere. Conversion runs entirely in your browser. The only third party this page talks to is our cookieless analytics provider, which counts visits — not content.

Your pasted data

Everything you paste, convert, copy, or download stays in your browser tab. The tool makes no network request containing your data — no upload endpoint exists. This is verifiable: open DevTools, watch the Network tab while converting, or read the open source. An automated test in the repository (test/no-network.test.js) fails the build if any network or storage API is ever added to the page outside the two script tags disclosed below.

Analytics (Plausible)

We load one analytics script from plausible.io, a privacy-focused, cookieless analytics service. It records pageviews and outbound link clicks with standard request metadata: truncated/hashed identifiers derived from your IP address and browser, referrer, and country. It sets no cookies, stores nothing on your device, and never sees what you paste into the tool. Plausible acts as our data processor; see the Plausible data policy. Legal basis under GDPR: legitimate interest in understanding aggregate usage. Because no cookies or persistent identifiers are used, no consent banner is required.

Site functionality (commandk.js)

The page also loads /commandk.js, the Tooly McToolface site-wide command palette (the Ctrl/⌘+K menu). It is served from this site, not a third party.

Settings links

The “Copy settings link” feature encodes your chosen formats and options in the URL fragment (the part after #). Fragments are never sent to any server. Your source data is never encoded into a link.

What we don’t do

  • No accounts, no sign-up, no email collection
  • No cookies, no fingerprinting
  • No advertising or ad-tech scripts
  • No sale or sharing of personal information (CCPA: we do not sell or share)

The one thing the tool can store (Pro)

If you activate the optional Pro feature (batch conversion), your license key is saved in your browser’s localStorage under the key ff-license so you stay activated between visits. The key is verified offline against a public key embedded in the page — activation makes no network call, and the stored key never leaves your device. Delete it any time by clearing site data for this site. If you never activate Pro, nothing is stored at all.

Your rights

Since we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to export or delete. For questions about aggregate analytics or anything on this page, email toolymctoolface@gmail.com. EU/UK visitors may also lodge a complaint with their supervisory authority.

Children

Format Flipper is a developer utility, not directed to children under 13, and collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes

If the tool’s data handling ever changes (for example, an optional feature that stores a setting on your device), this page and the tool’s FAQ will be updated first, before the change ships.
